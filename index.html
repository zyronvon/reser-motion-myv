How It Works

OUR
WORKFLOW

From raw footage to viral reel — here's our 4-step edit process.

01
FOOTAGE IN
Send us your raw clips via Google Drive or WhatsApp. We log every file into our project bin and prep the timeline.
02
CUT & PACE
Our editors make precise cuts, build the story arc, sync to music and engineer the pacing to hold attention till the last frame.
03
GRADE & VFX
Cinematic colour grading, motion graphics, animated captions, sound design and brand overlays — all applied to the locked cut.
04
EXPORT & DELIVER
Final render in vertical 9:16 optimised for Reels & TikTok. Delivered on time, every time — straight to your WhatsApp.
